Story behind the start-up

Even if you don’t have the perfect idea to begin with, you can likely adapt.

While being a native of Repulic of India, We also felt like this is not a homeland for embedded systems. And all the engineers from the feild were leaving for software industries, higher studies or for abroad. We founded mBEDed µSystems because we needed it in our own life. The start-up is Research and Development Firm involved in ranging from skeching out an idea to carve out a full-fledged product. We constantly innovating out the products and ideas for our cleints. mBEDed µSystems standardizes pricing to the whole RnD process to protect clients from unforeseen bumps or failures while giving service in wider array of products with all the emerging technologies to the clients. When you move through life fast as today, a little bit of disorganization is bound to happen. And we are expert in rattling through all those situations. That’s a win-win for everyone!
